Sunday Best

Robert Samuels


BOBBY TRENDZ!!!! YEAA THAT'S WHAT I LIKE TO BE CALLED!!! My Sunday Best is just that, THE BEST!! When Sunday roles around, I’m in my “glory.” My mood is usually captured in my stylish array of suits. God says “come as you are.” But I say “Come Correct.” I show up to praise his name, with poise and determination. Standing by this brick wall in my Salvatore Ferragamo jacket, Burberry plaid shirt, and Cole Hann poka-dot tie, I’m displaying my essence. I accessorized with my black fedora, wearing Burberry glasses, Michael Kors watch and Louis Vuitton briefcase. I’m ready for the runway or the boardroom. I stand as a confident black man. My motto is “God will make a way out of no way.” I’m my own model, and have my own career at Salvatore Ferragamo.

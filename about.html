ABOUT JWU TRENDSETTER

JWU Trendsetter is a fashion trend on campus to bring out student's colors and styles. With the trendsetter being on campus it gives students the opportunity to embrace their selves and show more of their colors. Shows more of self confidence, encourages them to stand out more, have more self esteem in what they desire. In addition, JWU Trendsetter sets out an exclusive edition to the campus to endorse students to feel comfortable in their own image and who they desire to be in life. Building up a good self-esteem and self-confidence build up leadership. As for some of us we get judge based on how you look which can destroy a person's image.
JWU Trendsetter sets out a comfort zone to feel free to give the students freedom to embrace their emotions on how they feel based on their style. Our vision is to have the student's feel comfortable as them in society.
That's what JWU Trendsetter loves bringing fashion trends into campus, to see how the students feel about their styles and to feel comfortable with their style. Don't feel shy to visit us, we will make you feel welcome because we are JWU family.
